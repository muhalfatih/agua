HERITAGE

1995

Muhammad Al Fatih, Surya Bonardo Sinaga, and Zainal F. Pratama realize that they've found the high quality water resource and begin the business.

1998

Their first factory was built in Yogyakarta, and the name of that company is AGUA.

2003

AGUA was Launched its first product in 950 ml glass bottle packaging that was produced from Yogyakarta factory. The price for each was Rp.75,-.

2008

AGUA started to built their second factory in Jakarta, in order to reach their customers in that area.

2011

AGUA made an innovation to their product by producing 220 ml PET bottle package. With this innovation, Agua enhanced their product quality and bacame safer to be consumed.

2013

AGUA won various awards in indonesian Best Brand Award. And keep improving their water quality

Today

Today AGUA is the best water company in indonesia cause they keep their quality and always make it better and better, that's why they have a lot of customers in all over indonesia.
